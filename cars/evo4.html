Evo 4.0

Achievements


  • Awarded Formula Student Award worth 3000 GBP by Institution of Mechanical Engineers (IMechE) out of 52 teams internationally
  • First Indian team to implement an Aluminium Honeycomb Monocoque chassis for weight reduction and greatly improved rigidity
  • High power Brushless DC Motors with Regenerative Braking were implemented to achieve 0-60 kmph in 2.18 sec
  • Carbon Fibre Underbody Diffuser developed using Full Car CFD simulations to provide more than 10 Kgs of downforce at the average speed for improved traction
  • Hybrid Planetary gearbox developed to achieve a higher gear ratio with 40% reduction in weight and smaller packaging
  • Custom Battery Management System (BMS) developed in-house to provide voltage monitoring, logging and temperature measurement for a 403.2 V, 6.66 kWh battery pack
