EvoX

Achievements


  • Awarded Formula Student Award worth 3000 GBP by Institution of Mechanical Engineers (IMechE) out of 82 teams internationally
  • Lightest car in the history of IIT Bombay Racing
  • In-house fabricated light weight Carbon Fiber bodyworks
  • India's first formula student race car to have implemented 3-D printed Titanium uprights
  • Successfully shifted to a 10-Inch rims design
  • Implemented the Aerodynamics package and built an in-house battery management system
  • High power Brushless DC Motors with Regenerative Braking were implemented to achieve 0-100 kmph in 2.88 sec
